Gall collection, measurement, and dissection protocol

Below we’ve linked the datasheets for Spring gall measurement and dissection. Data entry is done in two phases: 1) manual data entry while collecting data using the attached datasheet and 2) entering this data into the excel file linked below. An image of your paper data sheet and the digital file is then emailed to gall.size@utoronto.ca and submitted to us through the data submission form. Samples sorted by size and type are then shipped to the Zhang Lab for verification and genomic work.


If you are shipping from Canada, please email o.bronzomunich@gwu.edu to ensure all proper customs forms are submitted prior to shipment.


If you are dissecting galls after emergence, likely after rearing them out, we have also linked the key and datasheets below.

Data submission form

After you have collected your gall data, please use this form to submit your data file (.csv or .xls), an image of your paper datasheet, and necessary information on your samples (including tracking number for shipping) . If you have issues with the form, please email gall.size@utoronto.ca and o.bronzomunich@email.gwu.edu.
